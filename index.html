Partnering with Exhibit Houses to Create Unforgettable Experiences

With over 24 years of experience, Technical Communication Concepts (TCC) is your trusted partner in creating unforgettable experiences. We don’t just deliver AV solutions — we collaborate with you to bring your vision to life.

Our expertise spans cutting-edge displays, including modular systems, LED tiles, and 3D fans, offering a comprehensive range of tailored solutions for exhibits and live events. Grounded in creativity, ease of use, and the highest quality standards, our designs ensure seamless execution every time. Operating from key production hubs across the U.S., we proudly support partners nationwide, combining local insight with global innovation.

Modular Systems

Flexibility That Adapts to Your Vision

Our modular systems are designed to provide unmatched flexibility for your exhibit and event needs. These customizable solutions enable seamless integration and reconfiguration, allowing you to adapt your setups for various spaces and concepts effortlessly. Whether you're designing an intricate trade show booth or a dynamic stage for live events, our modular systems empower creativity while ensuring efficiency and ease of use.

LED Tiles

Brighten Every Event with Cutting-Edge Displays

Illuminate your exhibits and captivate your audience with our state-of-the-art LED tiles. Engineered for superior image quality and durability, our LED tiles deliver vibrant visuals that leave lasting impressions. Perfect for dynamic presentations, immersive backdrops, and branded messaging, these displays ensure your event stands out with clarity and brilliance.

3D Fans

Bring Your Vision to Life in Three Dimensions

Take your audience engagement to the next level with our innovative 3D fans. These mesmerizing displays project eye-catching holographic visuals that appear to float in mid-air, creating unforgettable moments for your audience. Ideal for product showcases, interactive exhibits, and brand storytelling, our 3D fans turn ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.
